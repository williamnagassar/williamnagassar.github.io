3D‑Printed Rotatable Amp Stand

Personal Design • PLA Material • 12 lb Load • ~280 g Print Mass

Overview

I designed a compact rotatable amp stand to raise and tilt a 12 lb guitar amplifier. The stand uses a low-profile wedge and captive pivot hardware to angle the amp upward toward the player. Internally, ribbing and tuned infill minimize material use while maintaining strength. The hub features a nyloc-secured pivot with an internal stop to hold angles securely.

Print & Assembly Notes

  • Nozzle: 0.4 mm, Layer Height: 0.2–0.28 mm
  • 4–5 perimeters, 10–20% grid/gyroid infill
  • Wedge printed on its side to maximize hinge strength
  • M5 countersunk bolt with nyloc nut for pivot
  • TPU feet or adhesive pads recommended for grip
