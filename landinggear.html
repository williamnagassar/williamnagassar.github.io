Landing Gear Mechanism Analysis

Mechanical Design Course • Ontario Tech University • Team of 5 • Role: Lead Analyst & Designer

Overview

This project focused on analyzing and designing a retractable landing gear mechanism for a light aircraft. The design had to withstand landing and taxi loads while providing sufficient ground clearance and smooth retraction kinematics.

Key Contributions

  • Led the structural and kinematic analysis to size the strut and linkage members.
  • Developed the detailed CAD model and assembly drawings in SolidWorks.
  • Calculated touchdown and side loads to verify safety factors above 1.5.
  • Coordinated team documentation and presentation.

Technical Specifications

ConfigurationTrailing arm, retractable main landing gear
Material6061-T6 Aluminum structure with 4140 steel pins
Design Load1.5 g touchdown at 1200 kg gross weight
Safety Factor≥ 1.5 on critical members
CAD SoftwareSolidWorks 2024
