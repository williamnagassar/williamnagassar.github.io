Exoskeleton Arm Design

I led the design of an exoskeleton arm driven by a linear actuator as part of a final project for a mechanical design course at Ontario Tech University. Working in a team of five, we took the project from initial sketches through to a functional prototype, which incorporated both 3D-printed components and laser-cut aluminum parts. I contributed with approximately 50% of the mechanical design, 100% of the strength analysis, and 30% of the machining/fabrication.
